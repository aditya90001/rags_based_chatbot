## SECTION: Placement Overview and Key Highlights
Source URL: Official AKGEC website, NIRF 2024-25, Multiple Education Portals

Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College (AKGEC) has a strong and consistent placement record. The Training and Placement (T&P) Cell organizes pre-placement training, workshops, mock interviews, and campus drives every year. AKGEC is accredited by NAAC with A++ grade. NBA accreditation is held for B.Tech branches: CSE, IT, ECE, EN, and ME.

Key recognition:
- Only engineering college in UP with NAAC A++ accreditation
- Academic Excellence Trophy for "Best Engineering College in UPTU" for two successive years
- Best LabVIEW Academy in India Award 2016 by National Instruments
- Best Technology Infrastructure of the Year Award 2018 by AI Council for Robotics and Automation
