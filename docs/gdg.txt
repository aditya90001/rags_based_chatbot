

About:
GDG on Campus AKGEC is a student-focused Google Developer Group on Campus. The group promotes learning, collaboration, and real-world development using Google technologies. It focuses on Web Development, Machine Learning, App Development, and Management. Its mission is to create a healthy developer culture on campus, support students in building real projects, grow skills in modern technologies, and promote peer learning, workshops, and community events.

Team Roles:
- Co-Organizers: Coordinate events and activities
- Core Team Members: Lead domain areas and projects
- Web Developers: Build websites and web applications
- App Developers: Build mobile or desktop applications
- Designers: UI/UX and graphics design
- Machine Learning Engineers: Implement ML solutions and AI projects
- Volunteers: Support community events and outreach
